Prefecture in Focus: Tottori

The capital city of Tottori Prefecture in the Chugoku region of Japan. Within Japan the city is best known for its sand dunes which are a popular tourist attraction, drawing visitors from outside the prefecture. Most of Tottori is located in the western part of the San'in Kaigan Geopark.

Featured Neighborhood: Kyoto’s Arashiyama

Arashiyama is a pleasant, touristy district in the western outskirts of Kyoto. It is particularly popular during the cherry blossom and fall color seasons.
